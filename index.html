    About

    I’m a second year Ph.D. student in Shanghai Jiao Tong University, where I also received B.S. and spent two years for M.S. in computer science, under the supervision of Prof. Cewu Lu and Yong-Lu Li. My research interests are data-efficient AI and fine-grained object concept reasoning for embodied agents.
